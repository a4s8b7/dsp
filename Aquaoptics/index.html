Aquaoptics are plants that light up. You can put them into a fish tank.
The best part about it is that no electricity goes into the water it's
all fiber optic. The Light source is were the Electricity ends, there is
a light bulb or high powered LED's that send the light into a fiber optic
cable. You can put that cable anywhere, allowing you to keep the light
source far away from any water. The fiber optic cable works like a light
pipe, bringing the light to were ever you want.
