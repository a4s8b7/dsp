Back in 2008 we came across a big box of American Flyer trains. The box had three complete train sets, a Stream line, a steam locomotive freighter, and a Diesel powered freighter. All of the trains were wrapped up in news paper from 1965, the trains themselves were from 1945. We restored the trains and put them up for auction, since they were antiques and better off in the hands of collectors. We would just play with them and cause damage. A special thanks goes out to the yahoo group S-scale for all their info and help.
