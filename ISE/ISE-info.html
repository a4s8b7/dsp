Overview button located near the lower right of the control screen. This button will bring up the overview page that shows the diffrent processes of the press. During machine operation the processor will highlight the current operation.


Allarms Page


Alarms Page located near the lower right of the control screen. If there are no Alarms or Errors then this page will be blank but as you recive Alarms such as "low Heat" or "Material dossage not complete" they will be listed on this Alarms page. The Alarms page will also show serious alarms such as machine malfuntions. If you find this is the case then you need to call tech support.
