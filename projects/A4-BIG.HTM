Check out how I've converted this Power Wheels Big Foot into a gas powered gocart. I started this
little project when a niehbor down the road from
me was throughing one out.After checking our
inventory of parts, I Whent and asked for the old
toy, Sure enough he gave it to me. Since then,
We've renforced the frame , added new
axles (frount and back), Took out all the old
electronics, and added a
gear box and drive shaft.



The cover is still the original cover, but it needed to be
repainted and modified. The
front end had to be completly cut out (except the
sides) to fit in the new
engine.The body monts had
to changed too (from the
cover to the frount axle). The
rest of the cover was sanded down and painted.


The Gokart can't take alot of abuse (due to its small
frame and Wheel base)
So I plan to use it as a show Gocart in /car
shows/fairs/and mybe some parades.

2004 update, this project has been put on hold since the drivetrains transmission has gone Kaput. Its currently buryed in the shed.
